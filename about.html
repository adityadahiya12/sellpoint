Connecting India's transport sector with trust and transparency since 2015

About Dahiya Truck Bazar

Dahiya Truck Bazar was founded with a singular mission: to revolutionize the way trucks are bought and sold in India. Born in Sonipat, Haryana, we've grown to become one of North India's most trusted platforms for commercial vehicle transactions, serving truck owners, fleet operators, and transport businesses across the region.

At our core, we're more than just a marketplace - we're a community built on reliability, efficiency, and transparency. We understand the challenges of the Indian transport sector, and we've created a platform that addresses the fragmentation and trust issues that have long plagued truck commerce.

Our Founder's Vision

"After 15 years in the commercial vehicle finance industry, I saw firsthand the challenges truck owners face when buying or selling their vehicles. The process was opaque, inefficient, and often left both parties dissatisfied. I founded Dahiya Truck Bazar to create a platform where transactions could happen with complete transparency, fair valuations, and genuine trust - the way business should be conducted in our industry."

Ranbir Singh Dahiya, our founder, brings unparalleled expertise from his tenure with Tata Motors Finance since 2006. His deep understanding of truck financing, bank settlements, and route optimization has shaped our company's commitment to providing comprehensive solutions that go beyond simple buying and selling.

Under his leadership, Dahiya Truck Bazar has grown from a local Sonipat operation to a regional leader, known for our integrity and customer-first approach in the North Indian transport sector.
